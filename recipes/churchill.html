Churchill the Beverage

Fresh and easy, non-alcohol drink

Ingredients

  • 250 ml Mineral water
  • 1 Amount Lemon
  • 2 Tablespoon Salt

Directions

  1. Squeeze the lemon into glass.
  2. Pour the mineral water, be careful with bubbles.
  3. Add salt.
  4. Additionally, you may add some ice.
