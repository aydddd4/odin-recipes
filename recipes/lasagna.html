Lasagna the Dish

In this classic lasagna recipe, sheets of pasta are layered with a cheesy filling, a rich meaty tomato sauce, and more cheese and then baked until bubbly and browned.

Ingredients

  • Cheese Filling
  • Meat
  • Sauce
  • Spinach (variation)

Steps

  1. Boil pasta:

    In a large pot of salted water boil lasagna noodles per the recipe below.

  2. Prepare meat sauce

    Cook sausage and beef with onion and garlic. Drain well, add the pasta sauce. Cook sausage and beef with onion and garlic. Drain well, add the pasta sauce.

  3. Combine cheese mixture

    Stir the cheese mixture together in a bowl.

  4. Layer & bake

    Layer the meat sauce and cheese mixture with lasagna noodles and bake until the top of the lasagna is golden brown.
